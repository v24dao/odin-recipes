Tomato Soup

Description

A rich and creamy tomato soup for the winter months. This recipe can be made with 10 minutes of prep and 30 minutes of cooking time!

Ingredients

  • 2 Onions (diced)
  • 10 cloves of Garlic (minced)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • Tinned Tomato (400gx4)
  • Vegetable stock (700ml)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Salt (2 tsp)
  • Chilli powder (1-2 tsp)
  • Basil (chopped)
  • Other herbs and spices (to taste)
  • Heavy Cream (300g)
